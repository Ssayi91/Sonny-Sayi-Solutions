Why These Solutions Matter for Your Business

Your Website Is Your Most Important Sales Tool

In Kenya, over 90% of customers research online before visiting a business. A professional website:

  • Builds instant trust—especially vs. competitors using only WhatsApp
  • Works 24/7 to capture leads while you sleep
  • Shows your full range of services or products (not just what fits in a bio)
  • Improves Google visibility for local searches
  • Reduces repetitive calls (“Do you offer X?”) with clear info online

A well-built site pays for itself in just a few new clients.

Your Network Is Your Operational Backbone

When your internet drops during a payment or your files won’t load, you lose time—and customers. A professional network:

  • Separates staff traffic from guest Wi-Fi for security
  • Ensures POS systems, WhatsApp, and cloud tools work reliably
  • Prevents data leaks from unsecured connections
  • Scales as you add cameras, printers, or new staff

For under 20,000 Kshs, you can eliminate daily tech frustrations for months.

Your Customers Are Your Greatest Asset—Don’t Lose Them

Most Kenyan SMEs lose 60–80% of leads because they lack a system to follow up. A CRM helps you:

  • Remember who asked for a quote last month
  • Send birthday or service reminders automatically
  • Track which marketing channel brings the best clients
  • Upsell to past customers

You don’t need complexity—just a simple, reliable way to stay top-of-mind.
