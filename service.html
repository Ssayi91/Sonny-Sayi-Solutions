  • Builds instant trust—especially vs. competitors using only WhatsApp

  • Shows your full range of services or products (not just what fits in a bio)

When your internet drops during a payment or your files won’t load, you lose time—and customers. A professional network:

  • Scales as you add cameras, printers, or new staff

For under 20,000 Kshs, you can eliminate daily tech frustrations for months.
