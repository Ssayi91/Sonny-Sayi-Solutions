Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer payment installment plans? Popular

Yes, we offer flexible installment plans for projects over KES 20,000. Our standard plan includes:

  • 50% deposit to start the project
  • 30% payment upon completion of milestones
  • 20% final payment upon project delivery

We can customize payment schedules based on your project size and duration. Just indicate your interest in the contact form above.

How long does a typical website project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but here's our typical schedule:

  • Basic websites: 1-2 weeks
  • E-commerce sites: 4-6 weeks
  • Custom web applications: 6-12 weeks

We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and project scope definition.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer several support options tailored to your needs:

  • Basic Support: KES 3,000/month (security updates, minor fixes)
  • Premium Support: KES 8,000/month (includes content updates, performance optimization)
  • Enterprise Support: Custom packages for large businesses

All projects come with 30 - 60 days (depends on the project/website type) of free support after launch.
