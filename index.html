Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to design and launch a website?

The timeline varies based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while e-commerce sites may take 4-6 weeks. We work efficiently to deliver quality results while respecting your timeline.

Can you help fix an existing website or upgrade it?

Absolutely! We specialize in website renovations, upgrades, and troubleshooting. We can enhance functionality, improve design, optimize performance, or fix any technical issues your current site may have.

Do you provide hosting and domain registration?

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting solutions tailored to your website's needs. We can register domains on your behalf or transfer existing domains. Our hosting packages include security, backups, and technical support.

What networking solutions do you offer for offices?

We provide end-to-end networking solutions including structured cabling, wireless access points, network security, VPN setup, server configuration, and ongoing maintenance. We design networks that scale with your business.
