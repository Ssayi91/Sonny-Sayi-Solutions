Warning Signs

When You Need IT Restructuring

Instability signals structural gaps. If you experience these issues, intervention is required.

Frequent Outages

Network downtime occurs regularly, disrupting operations and productivity.

Poor Coverage

Wi-Fi signals are weak or inconsistent across your office space.

Unclear Backups

Data backup processes are manual, inconsistent, or untested.

Informal Access

Shared passwords and no role-based access control.

Unmanaged Devices

No centralized device management or update system.

Growth Pains

Infrastructure hasn't scaled with business expansion.
